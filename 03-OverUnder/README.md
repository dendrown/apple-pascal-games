## OVERUNDER
In _Apple_Pascal_Games_, Hergert &  Kalash write:
> The player chooses a number between 2 and 12 and places a bet.  Two dice are rolled.
> If the number chosen is the same as the  roll of the dice, the player wins four times
> the amount of the bet.  Failing an exact match between the "lucky" number and the throw
> of the dice, the player can still win the amount of the bet if the number and the roll
> of the dice are both under or both over 7.  Otherwise, the amount of the bet is lost.
> 
> You begin this game with $500 in you pocket, and you can continue playing as long
> as your money and stamina hold out.
